Lifey – Account & Data Deletion

App Name:

Lifey

Developer:

DevNestApps Team

How to Request Deletion:

  1. Send an email to devnestapps@gmail.com with subject: "Request to Delete My Lifey Account"
  2. Include:
    • Your registered email
    • Your display name (if any)
    • A message confirming your deletion request
  3. We’ll confirm and delete within 3–5 business days.

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account (Firebase Auth)
  • Uploaded Data
  • Saved items linked to your UID

Data Retention:

We don’t retain personal data post deletion, except anonymized metrics or as required by law.
